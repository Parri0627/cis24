Survival Guide

Course in Summer 2024

June 24th - July 1st

Join the JedAIs on this epic educational adventure and earn your certificate in AI-Enhanced Project Management while exploring one of Spain's most vibrant destinations. May the force be with you!

Everyone has heard about Artificial Intelligence and how helpful it can be. In this course you'll collaborate with a crew of fellow padawans from across the galaxy to scope. We will train you in the latest project management techniques and strategies using cutting-edge AI technology.

Learn how to scope, plan, schedule, and monitor projects. Master project management tools, and virtual collaboration methods and gain hands-on experience running an actual project from start to finish.

But it's not all work and no play! Valencia is one of Spain's most vibrant and lively cities. Spend your weekends exploring the winding streets, lounging on the sun-kissed beaches, and sampling mouthwatering paella. Enjoy exciting nightlife and dancing until dawn with new friends from around the galaxy. This course doesn't just sharpen your mind - it feeds your soul! You'll make memories to last a lifetime in one of the most beautiful destinations in the universe.
